## Toasts

✅ Good:
> **Settings saved**
> Your settings were saved successfully.

✅ Good:
> **Segment archived**
> You can restore it from the Settings page.

❌ Bad:
> Success! Your changes were submitted to the server!

❌ Bad:
> Settings

## Alerts

✅ Good:
> **This segment is used in other dashboards**
> Removing it will affect saved views for you and your team.

✅ Good:
> **Guide is disabled**
> This guide is missing a step and can't be published.

❌ Bad:
> Warning: You must fix this.

❌ Bad:
> Something's wrong!

## Confirmations

✅ Good:
> **Delete dashboards?**
> You're about to delete these 12 dashboards. They'll be removed for anyone with access and can't be recovered.

✅ Good:
> **Leave this team?**
> You'll lose access to shared dashboards and settings.

❌ Bad:
> Are you sure?

❌ Bad:
> This will be deleted. OK?

## Tooltips

✅ Good:
> Choose whether to measure by individual groups or grouped accounts. This impacts how onboarding success is calculated.

✅ Good:
> This area only shows results from verified segments.

❌ Bad:
> You can hover to get more info.

❌ Bad:
> This is a tooltip.

## Empty states

✅ Good:
> **No saved views**
> Try saving a view to make these filters easy to revisit.

✅ Good:
> **Start by tagging Features**
> Tag elements in your app to begin collecting insights.

❌ Bad:
> Nothing to see here!

❌ Bad:
> Yikes, you haven't saved anything yet.

## Error messages

✅ Good:
> We couldn't upload your file. Try again or choose a different format.

✅ Good:
> This field is required.

❌ Bad:
> Uh-oh! That didn't work.

❌ Bad:
> Invalid input error 093XF.

## CTA buttons

✅ Good:
- Save changes
- Delete segment
- Create guide

❌ Bad:
- Yes
- OK
- Submit

Use this file to align examples across projects or when calibrating AI-generated UX copy.
