## Overview

Pendo sounds like a knowledgeable, friendly guide. We aim to be clear, useful, and human — avoiding filler, formality, and jargon. We're warm and confident, not robotic or salesy.

Our voice stays consistent across the product, whether the content is written by a person or generated by AI. Tone shifts depending on the user's context, but should always reflect the same core values.

---

## Brand foundation

All product content — especially in-product microcopy — should reflect Pendo's brand attributes and personality.

### Mission
**To elevate the world's experience with software.**

### Brand promise
**Software that makes your software better.**

### Brand attributes
- **Dependable** — Supportive, expert, and always delivering value
- **Passionate** — We cheer our customers on and help them succeed
- **Productive** — Fast, focused, and useful with clear context
- **Honest** — Transparent, trustworthy, and never misleading

### Brand personality
- More simple than technical
- A blend of serious and friendly
- More modern than traditional
- Always literal, not abstract
- More bright than subtle

Aim for thoughtful clarity, not cleverness.

---

## Voice principles

We aim to:
- **Be clear.** Use plain, direct language that's easy to understand — especially for a global audience.
- **Be useful.** Focus on what the user can do and what outcomes they'll see.
- **Be human.** Sound warm, confident, and natural.

Avoid:
- Jargon or overly formal language
- Filler and vague encouragement ("just," "easily," "seamlessly")
- Excessive positivity, cheerleading, or robotic phrasing

---

## Tone by context

| Context | Tone | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Getting started | Encouraging | Help users feel confident and oriented |
| Product exploration | Informative | Focus on what's possible, with examples if needed |
| Troubleshooting | Reassuring | Be calm, specific, and non-blaming |
| Task-based guides | Actionable | Focus on user steps and outcomes |
| Release notes | Neutral | Describe changes factually, with user value included |

---

## For AI-generated content

All AI-generated content should follow the same voice and tone principles as human-written content — with a few extra considerations:

- Use natural, conversational phrasing that feels like it came from a real person
- Be transparent when the AI creates or suggests something (if relevant to the user)
- Be honest and helpful. If the AI can't do something, acknowledge it clearly and guide the user forward

### Pronoun guidance

- Use **"I"** when the AI is acting on the user's behalf
  - "I created this report for you."
- Use **"we"** when referring to Pendo's broader capabilities
  - "We help you understand user behavior."

---

## Final check

Before publishing any product content (human or AI-written), ask:
- Is it clear on the first read?
- Is it useful to the user?
- Does it sound like something a real person would say?
- Will it make sense to someone reading in a different country or language?
